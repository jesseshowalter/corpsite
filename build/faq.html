Hobnob Basics

What is Hobnob?

Hobnob is an iOS and Android app that helps you create a stunning invitation design and send it to your guests by text message or email. Your guests can RSVP simply by replying to your text message or email, or can visit the web invite to get more details, chat, upload photos, and more - no app required! With the app, you can chat with your guests, see their RSVPs, collect photos, and send updates.

How do I use hobnob?

First, search for Hobnob on the iOS or Android app store or click here. Once you've installed Hobnob, get started by adding details about your event and selecting a design (or uploading your own). Once you're happy with your design, select your guests and send the invite by text message or email. Your guests will start RSVPing soon after receiving their invite, and you'll be well on your way to hosting a great party!

Can I send my invites by text message using Hobnob?

Yes! With Hobnob, you can choose to send your invitations by either text message or email. People who you invite via text message will receive a text message with your invitation design, your event name, and a link to your web invite. They can simply reply "Yes" to their text message, or click through to visit the web invite to get more details and RSVP. It's that simple!

Can I send my invites by email using Hobnob?

Yes! With Hobnob, you can choose to send your invitations by either text message or email. People who you invite via email will receive an email with your invitation design, your event details, and all of the other information they need to RSVP. They can simply reply "Yes" to their invitation, or click through from the email to visit the web invite to get more details and RSVP. Easy!

Sending Invites

Can I send invites through text and email?

Yes! You can choose the best way to invite each contact. Select any mobile number or email that you have saved in your address book to invite them to an event.

Why are text invites not sent from my number?

Guests added from the main add guests screen will get a text from Hobnob’s shortcode number (462 662). This allows guests to just reply to the text with “Yes” or “No” to RSVP and we keep track of all of the RSVPs for you. We can understand your concern that your guests may not recognize the number, which is why we make sure to include the host's name on the invite so your guests immediately know who it is from.

What happens when a guest replies to the text or email invite?

If a guest responds to the text they receive with an answer that is not an RSVP we forward that message to the host as a private message.

Can Hobnob be used to send invites to international numbers?

Currently, Hobnob is only available in the US.

Why don’t I see all my contacts on my Android phone?

You may have a filter on your contacts. To turn off the filter:

  1. Go into your Contacts app and press the menu button in the top left
  2. Press “Display Options”
  3. Select the gmail account that your contacts are being synced with, and make sure all groups have check marks. That will turn off all filters.

On Samsung devices it’s a little different:

  1. Go to: More > Settings > Contacts to Display
  2. Your settings should be set to “All contacts”
  3. Or use “Customized list” and turn on all the options to enable more contacts to be visible from within the app

Location

What if my location doesn't show up when searching Google Places?

If your location name doesn't match any locations returned by the Google Places search within the app, you can enter your own custom location name to use instead. This type of location won't include a map on your invite.

Guests

Can I hide my guest list?

Yes, if you go to the event in the app (from the My Invites screen, tap your event), scroll down to the bottom and you'll see a section called "Event Settings". There, under Host Preferences, you'll see an option to turn off Guest List. You can re-enable it at any time.

How do I change my guest's RSVP?

As the host, you can change your guest’s RSVP from the guest list. Tap (iPhone), or long tap (Android) on the Y, N or ? icon next to your guest’s name to see options to change their RSVP.

How do I delete a guest?

To delete a guest on iPhone, swipe left on their name on the guest list to see the option to delete. On Android, long tap on the guest’s name to see the option to remove the guest. Guests will not be notified that they have been deleted.

How do I download my guest list?

As the host, you can download your guest list. Open your invite to the guest list and tap the download icon (down arrow) to see options of where to send your file (ie. email it to yourself). The guest list will be exported as a file and can be opened with Microsoft Excel or Google Docs.

Co-Hosting

Can my event have more than one host?

Yes! With Hobnob, any number of people can host a single event together. Whoever creates the event can add co-hosts in the same step as inviting guests. Co-Hosts can invite people to the party from their own address books, just like you can! Now every party is better with friends!

How do I add a co-host?

Open your invite to your guest list. On iPhone, swipe left on any guest to see option to add a co-host. On Android, long press on any guest’s name to add a co-host. Co-hosts can help you manage your guest list by adding or removing guests and they will be listed as a co-host in the guest list.

Chat

Can I chat with my guests and co-hosts?

Of course! Hobnob allows everyone invited to the party to chat as a group (using the app or the web), or to message each other privately (using the app). Best of all, you can continue to chat after the party so you can keep in touch between events and plan your next party together!

Who receives chat messages?

Guests who have RSVP’d yes or are undecided will receive chat messages via text message. Each guest can adjust their chat notification preferences in the settings section of Messages on the web invite or by replying OFF to any chat message

Can I turn off Guest Chat?

Yes, you can turn off guest chat in the app. Go to your Event Settings and toggle off Guest Chat.

Direct Messages

Who can receive a direct message?

Anyone! Direct messages work best if both users have the Hobnob app. For each direct message the recipient will get a push notification and be able to see all their messages in the messages tab.

What if someone doesn't have the Hobnob app? Can I send them a direct message?

If you want to direct message someone who doesn’t have the Hobnob app, we will send them the first message via text. This let’s them know you want to chat and they just need to download the app to continue the conversation.

How can I submit photos for an event?
