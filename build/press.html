How It Works

1Quick & Easy

Hobnob gets everyone together instantly by chatting or sending an invite via text or email. Stunning templates make anyone an instant designer so you can make an invite in just seconds..

2Message people you care about

Hobnob’s direct and group messaging features not only allow hosts to send event updates to guests via text messages, but also allows guests to follow up with other guests they met at these events.

3Effortless

Hobnob takes all the stress out of planning. Text reminders are sent automatically so you get ridiculously fast RSVPs, guests know where to go, and you don’t need to ask your guests to share photos.
