About

Hobnob is...
actually social.

We love making it easier for people to get together in real life so they're actually social again. “Social” just isn’t social anymore. Our mission it to leverage technology to help people spend more time in real life with the people they care about most. We do this by helping you create exclusive mini social networks for all the important people and moments in your life.

Company Values

1Integrity

We trust each other, hold each other accountable, and take responsibility for our actions. Everything we do is built on a foundation of mutual respect, humility, and honesty so we can all thrive.

2Curioisty

Our team is insatiably hungry to learn - about what we can do better, how we can make our users happier, and about the best tools to accomplish these goals. We love the journey, the effort, and the reward of pushing boundaries.

3Diversity

We are proud that our team mirrors our user base with varied backgrounds, educational training, and family structures. This isn't just lip service, with a latino male and two female co-founders, we live and breathe it.

4Perspective

To celebrate life, you have to experience it. To build something for the world, you have to see it. We want our team to have a well‑rounded life, celebrating moments both personal and professional.

5Authenticity

Our mission is to encourage more genuine interaction, with authentic relationships in real‑life. We can only do this by being real ourselves.
