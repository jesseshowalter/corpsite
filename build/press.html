How do we do this?

1Quick & Easy

Hobnob allows you to quickly create and send out your invitations by turning all your event details into a stunning invitation that can be sent directly from the app to phone numbers (as text messages) and email addresses (as emails).

2Automated Notifications

Too busy to send reminders to your guests? No problem. Hobnob sends automatic reminders to guests on your behalf about location, RSVP and sharing photos. Guests receive these reminders via text message.

3Message people you care about

Hobnob’s direct and group messaging features not only allow hosts to send event updates to guests via text messages, but also allows guests to follow up with other guests they met at these events.
